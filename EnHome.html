Biography

My supervisor is Prof. Jungang Xu. I received my B.S. degree in Internet of things engineering from School of Automation, Chongqing University of Posts and Telecommunications in 2018.

Research Interests: Auto Machine Learning (AutoML) and Computer Vision (CV).
